About Our College



The D Y Patil School of Engineering is one of the most prestigious academic group in the state of Maharastra which comes under the aegis of the D Y Patil Technical Campus. D Y Patil School of Engineering, Lohegaon (DYPSOE), Pune, was established by the honorable Dr. Ajeenkya D Y Patil, Chairman of D Y Patil Group. The Institute offers two Diploma programs.


D Y Patil School of Engineering , situated in "Oxford of the East" Pune, has proven to be "avant-grade" in the field of Engineering over past few years in this region.


The Institute,located in Lohegaon Pune, is spread over an area of 100 acres interspersed with various sport facility, wellness center a world class auditorium and lash green campus. Pune city is well connected with Mumbai, Nagpur and Delhi & by regular flights and is on the main railway line route. The institute is 10km from Pune railway station and 7km from lohegaon airport. Many industries, such as those of Software, Mechanical, Automobile, stell etc., are located in the vicinity of the institute giving it a unique advantages for industry-institute interaction in various discipline of engineering.
